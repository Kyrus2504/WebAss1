Primary School Life

My Time in CSPS

From K-6 I attended Carlton South Primary School in, you guessed it, Carlton, Sydney. My Primary school life was nothing special, with me completing my time there with no issues. I enjoyed myself back then by playing cricket during the summer season and rugby during the winter season. I also partook in the debating club which ended up winning undefeated that year under our command.


Notable Achievements

  • Undefeated in the Year 6 Debating Season
  • Finalist for the Number Crunchers competition for the St. George area in Year 5
  • Rugby Player during the winter season during Year 6

High School & Formative Years

High School Overview

High School was an interesting experience as I had to learn about the real world, and at the end, decide on my future career path. The first years of high school were nothing special, with me keeping up with all my subjects. The fun part were the sports I took part in. During Year 7-11 I was part of my schools Rowing team, where I acted as the coxswain, and ended up getting to attend the Head of the River in my final year, which is just a large competition. In winter, I took part in Cross Country running between years 7-10 and then Rock Climbing for the last 2 years.

All of those experiences shaped who I am today
